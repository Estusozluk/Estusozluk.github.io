What is ESTÜ sözlük ?

Estü sözlük is a website where people share thoughts and get information under certain conditions. You can write entries and other people can share their thoughts about that topic.

Features

Aim

A website for information purposes that anyone can subscribe to and read.

Pricing

A free website open to everyone. Revenues are generated from advertisements shown to members.

Writers

Members who can enter entries approved by the moderators.

Readers

Anyone, whether a member or not, can read it for free.
